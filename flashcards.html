How to Use the Flashcards



Using DevMaster flashcards are simple and intuitive. Start by selecting a topic—like HTML, CSS, or JavaScript—and choose your skill level: beginner, intermediate, or advanced. Once you're in, flip through the flashcards one by one to test your knowledge. Click to reveal the answer, and mark whether you got it right or wrong to help track your progress. You can filter cards based on what you've mastered or need to review, and revisit them anytime to reinforce your learning. Whether you're studying on your desktop or mobile, the cards are always ready when you are—just flip, learn, and level up.
